MEMORY CHALLENGE

How to Play:

You will be shown 30 words on the screen.

You have 2 minutes to memorize as many words as possible.

After the time is up, you will be asked to type all the words you remember.

Separate recalled words with spaces or new lines.

Earn XP (5 per word) and improve your Intelligence stat based on how many words you correctly recall.

Unlock the Memory Master achievement by recalling all 30 words!
